👔 Dress Code (Strictly Enforced)
Men: Full trousers + shirt. NO shorts or bermudas allowed inside. Women: Saree or churidar/salwar kameez. No sleeveless, no western wear.
📱 No Phones Inside Sanctum
Mobile phones & cameras are NOT allowed inside the main sanctum sanctorum. Deposit at the cloak room (₹5–₹20) before entering.
🎫 SSD Ticket (Recommended)
Book Special Entry Darshan (SSD) tickets at tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in — ₹300/person. Wait: 2–4 hrs. Free darshan wait can be 8–18 hrs. Book 2–4 weeks in advance!
🚌 Tirumala Transport
Only TTD buses go up Tirumala hill. Depart from Alipiri bus stand. Non-AC: ₹36, AC: ₹56. Run 24 hours. Start early (4–5 AM) to beat crowds.
🍛 Free Prasadam Meal
TTD Annadanam provides FREE meals to all pilgrims — rice, sambar, rasam, curd on banana leaves. One of India's largest free feeding halls. Don't miss it!
🍬 Tirupati Laddu
Collect laddu prasadam coupons at TTD counters — ₹50/piece. Limited per person per visit. Buy as many as permitted — they're famous!
🚂 Book Trains Early on IRCTC
Always book return trains via IRCTC app. Train 22870 (MAS VSKP) for Satya & Surya fills up fast. Check Tatkal quota if regular quota is full.
✈️ Web Check-in for Flight
Prathyusha & Venky: Do web check-in the night before (March 23 night). Arrive at MAA T1 by 2:30 PM for 4:30 PM flight. Security takes 30–45 mins.
🛵 Scooter Rental in Pondicherry
Best way to explore Pondi is on rented scooters. ₹300–₹500/day from shops near White Town hotels. Need a driving license. Cycle: ₹100–₹150.
🔒 Safety & Scam Alert
Beware of touts near Tirupati offering "instant darshan" — these are scams. Book ONLY through official TTD website. Use OLA/Uber instead of negotiating with autos.
☀️ Beat the Heat (March ~34°C)
South India in March is hot and humid. Carry reusable water bottle, ORS sachets, sunscreen. Plan outdoor activities before 11 AM and after 4 PM.
