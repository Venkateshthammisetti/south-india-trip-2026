👔 Dress Code
Men: Full trousers + shirt. NO shorts allowed. Women: Saree or churidar/salwar. No sleeveless or western wear inside temple.
📱 No Phones Inside
Mobile phones & cameras NOT allowed inside the sanctum. Deposit at cloak room (₹5–₹20) before entering.
🎫 SSD Ticket (Must Book)
Book at tirupatibalaji.ap.gov.in — ₹300/person. Wait time: 2–4 hrs (vs 8–18 hrs free darshan). Book 2–4 weeks in advance!
🚌 Tirumala Bus
Only TTD buses go up to Tirumala. Alipiri bus stand. Non-AC ₹36, AC ₹56. Run 24 hrs. Start early 4–5 AM to beat crowds.
🍛 Free Annadanam Meal
TTD provides FREE meals to all pilgrims — rice, sambar, rasam, curd on banana leaves. One of India's largest free feeding programs!
✈️ Web Check-in
Prathyusha & Venky: Web check-in night before (Mar 23). Arrive MAA T1 by 2:30 PM for the 4:30 PM flight. Security takes 30–45 mins.
🛵 Scooter Rental Pondi
Best way to explore Pondicherry! ₹300–₹500/day per scooter. Need driving license. Cycles ₹100–₹150/day.
🔒 Scam Alert Tirupati
Beware of touts near Tirupati offering "instant darshan" — these are scams. Book ONLY via TTD official website. Use Ola/Uber for all transport.
☀️ Beat the Heat
March in South India is ~34°C hot and humid. Carry water bottle, ORS sachets, sunscreen. Plan outdoor activities before 11 AM and after 4 PM.
📲 Must-Have Apps
IRCTC Rail Connect · Redbus · Ola/Uber · Google Maps (download offline!) · TTD Official App · Zomato · PhonePe / GPay
